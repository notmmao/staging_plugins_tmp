A) Installation and Activation - top

1. Upload zip file to wordpress plugin folders and activate it.



2. New menu will be available under "Woocommerce"



B) Apply Discounts to role group - top



1. Here you can apply discount percentange to each roles. Lets suppose the original price of a product is $100, we apply 10% discount to customer role and save the change. All the users with customer role will be seen the price as $90 (10% discounted to the original price).

2. Avoid % symbol while enter the discount. Use only digits (ex: 10).

3. Leave the discount field empty if you dont want to apply discount to any role group.



C) Format Price - top



1. Tick the checkbox to apply round price. Lets suppose the original price of the product is $85 and we apply 10% discount. The output will be $76.5, tick the 'Show rounded price' option to show $77.



D) Create New Role - top



1. Here you can create unlimited number of roles. Type a role name and click 'create role' button, the role will be created immediately and shown under 'roles and discount' area.



E) Delete Role - top



1. Pick any unwanted role and remove instantly. You can not delete administrator role. Always be careful before removing the roles, the plugin will not ask for any confirmation.



F) Exclude Category - top



1. Select number of categories to exclude from discounted price. Any products belongs to these categories will not be applied with discounts.



G) Tax Exemption - top



1. Allow tax exemption for the selected country(s) based on the role(s) you selected.
